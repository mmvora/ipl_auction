1 Entry & Auction

Each participant must pay $200 HKD to enter the league.

  • Payments must be settled before the auction begins.
  • Each team receives a purse of 100 Crore to spend during the auction.

The auction will take place via video call, using a custom-built auction software that provides real-time updates on player purchases, remaining budget, and squad details.

The auction will be held on Saturday 28th February from 11:00 PM HKT onwards.

2 Squad Composition

Each participant must build a squad of 11 players, adhering to the following minimums:

  • Batters: minimum 2
  • Bowlers: minimum 2
  • Wicketkeeper: minimum 1
  • Remaining slots are free to fill as you choose

While there is no limit on the number of overseas players you can purchase during the auction, please keep in mind that no more than 4 overseas players can participate in an IPL match.

Once a team of 11 has been selected during the auction, no player transfers can be made and you will keep the same team through both the league and playoff stages.

4 Player Replacements

Each team receives 1 Injury Card per season.

  • The card may be used when a player in your squad has been officially ruled out for the remainder of the season.
  • You can only use it once ‚Äî it does not refresh.
  • Replacements must be like-for-like by position (e.g. keeper for keeper, bowler for bowler).
  • Injury Cards can be traded between teams.
  • All replacement decisions are at the sole discretion of the League Master.

6 Trading Window

Captains & Vice Captains

  • No leadership changes are permitted during the season.
  • The only exception: if your Captain or Vice Captain is confirmed injured, you may swap that role once.
  • You may not trade your Captain or Vice Captain.

Trading Window

  • The trading window opens after the first ball of the IPL season and closes when the playoffs begin.
  • During this window you may trade any eligible player with a player from another team.
  • Inform the League Master of any trade at least 6 hours before the next game ‚Äî otherwise the trade takes effect on the following matchday.
  • Points accrued by a player will not transfer with the trade.
