1 Entry & Auction

Each participant must pay $200 HKD to enter the league.

  • Payments must be settled before the auction begins.
  • Upon entry, each participant will receive 100 Cr to spend in the auction.

The auction will take place via video call, using a custom-built auction software that provides real-time updates on player purchases, remaining budget, and squad details.

The auction will be held on Saturday 28th February from 11:00 PM HKT onwards.

4 Player Replacements
  • A replacement may be allowed if a player in a participant's squad is ruled out of the IPL due to injury.
  • A replacement is only permitted if the real IPL franchise signs an official replacement for the injured player.
  • The replacement must be the exact player brought in by the IPL team.
  • All replacement decisions are at the sole discretion of the League Master.

6 Mid-Season Transfers & Drafts

Captains & Vice Captains

  • Your Captain and Vice Captain may not be changed throughout the season, except for injuries that have ruled them out of the whole season.
  • You may also not transfer your Captain or Vice Captain.

Pre-Season Trades

  • Before the first toss of the season, you may choose to trade any player with a player from another team.
  • Please inform the League Master of the trade as soon as possible.

In-Season Transfers

  • During the season you may inform the League Master of any player swaps you want to make.
  • You must inform him at least 6 hours before the next game, otherwise the transfer will occur on the following day.
  • Points accrued by a player will not transfer to another team.

Mid-Season Player Draft

  • A Mid-Season Player Draft will be held after Match 36 of the season, where all unsold players will be available through a draft. You may select up to 2 players.
  • A randomizer will decide pick order, weighted towards the team with the lowest points in the Fantasy League.
